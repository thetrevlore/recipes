Ingredients:
- 1 Strip Sirloin Steak
- Pappy's Seasoning
- Butter

Directions:
1. Set steak on the counter an hour or two before you cook it so that
it is room temperature when you start cooking it.
2. Rub Pappy's Seasoning on both sides of the steak.
3. Turn burner on high and melt butter in pan. Let it get as hot as possible without hurting anything.
4. Place steak in pan, cook each side for 5 minutes.
5. Eat the delicious meat!
